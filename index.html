Projects, so far...

You Can Solo -- guitar learning mobile app

This app helps the beginner, even advanced player, dive deeper into music theory and their own creativity by generating an unlimited amount of chord progressions accompanied by scales patterns, triads and other techniques to get the player playing with purpose! You Can Solo will help any musician struggling to play different ideas over the chords they already know and love. MIDI jam tracks, with authentic instruments, are available for free with more instruments coming soon. Includes over 100+ unique scales, patterns and melodic devices.
